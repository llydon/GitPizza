# Fast Style Pizza
For when you don't have any time to make pizza or go to the store.

## Ingredients
- Domino's
- Wine

## Directions
1. Pick up the your phone
2. Open Domino's app
3. Pick out pizza
4. Pay for Pizza
5. Wait for the pizza to arrive
6. Drink wine
7. When doorbell rings, bring in pizza
8. Eat pizza and drink the rest of the wine
